Doppelganger Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES No Powers of Your Own Whenever you gain a power card, instead of keeping it, you must give it to another spirit. You have no cards of your own. If you choose to gain a major power, the spirit you give it to must forget a power card to receive it.

Your Copycat power may not be repeated in any way.

When you take presence off your presence track, you may take it off of anywhere. Reshape One's Image At the beginning of each spirit phase, you may copy target {spirit}'s Special Rules and use them in addition to your own. You cannot choose the same spirit 2 turns in a row unless there is no other choice. For each card play you have this turn, you may copy a power from a spirit with {presence} on the same board as you. You may copy an innate power or a power card. When you copy a power card, you must pay energy cost of the card, and you receive any elements listed on that card. Each time you copy a power, you must copy from a spirit you have not yet copied a power from yet if possible. Once per turn while using this power, if you have at least 4 of any one element type, you may sacrifice one of your {presence} to to allow a spirit you copied a power of add 1 {presence} in the land you sacrificed your presence. If the targeted power grants defense, it grants an additional +1 defense per {earth} {water} If the targeted power moves tokens, it moves an additional token of that type per {animal} {air} If the targeted power deals damage, it deals 1 more damage per {fire} {sun} If the targeted power adds {beast}/{disease}/{strife}/{wilds}/{badlands}, it adds 1 more token of that type per {plant} {moon}
