Doppelganger Greg Cole SPECIAL RULES No Powers of Your Own Whenever you gain a power card, instead of keeping it, you must give it to another spirit. You have no cards of your own. If you choose to gain a major power, the spirit you give it to must forget a power card to receive it.

Your Copycat power may not be repeated in any way. For each card play you have this turn, you may copy a power from a spirit with {presence} on the same board as you. You may copy an innate power or a power card. When you copy a power card, you must pay energy cost of the card, and you receive any elements listed on that card. Each time you copy a power, you must copy from a spirit you have not yet copied a power from yet if possible. Once per turn while using this power, if you have at least 3 of any one element type, you may pay 3 energy to allow one of the spirits whose power your copied to add a {presence} in one of your lands. If the targeted power grants defense, it grants an additional +1 defense per {earth} {water} If the targeted power moves tokens, it moves an additional token of that type per {air} {animal} If the targeted power deals damage, it deals 1 more damage per {fire} {sun} If the targeted power adds {beast}/{disease}/{strife}/{wilds}/{badlands}, it adds 1 more token of that type per {plant} {moon}
